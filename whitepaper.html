1. Introduction

BleuToken is a revolutionary blockchain project aimed at blending meme culture with meaningful charitable contributions. Built on Binance Smart Chain (BSC), BleuToken ($BLEU) is designed to bring real-world impact to dog welfare organizations globally while giving the crypto community an opportunity to support an inspiring cause.

2. Vision and Mission

Vision: To create a world where blockchain empowers global dog charities while entertaining the crypto community.

Mission:

  • To deliver a scalable and user-friendly cryptocurrency.
  • To allocate funds transparently to dog charities.
  • To use raised liquidity to grow our presence on major exchanges.

3. The Problem

Globally, millions of dogs are neglected or suffer due to a lack of resources in shelters and charity organizations. Despite advancements in charity technologies, there is little to no transparency in how funds are distributed or used.

4. Our Solution

BleuToken introduces a transparent, blockchain-based system where every transaction contributes to meaningful causes. With smart contract integration, 1% of every token sale is sent directly to the charity wallet, ensuring traceability and accountability. Raised funds during the pre-sale and subsequent transactions will also be used to secure listings on major cryptocurrency exchanges, helping to expand the reach and liquidity of BleuToken.

5. Tokenomics

Token Details:

  • Token Name: BleuToken
  • Ticker Symbol: $BLEU
  • Network: Binance Smart Chain
  • Total Supply: 1 Billion $BLEU

Distribution:

  • Charity Wallet: 1% of all transactions.
  • Maintenance Wallet: 0.02% of all transactions for minimal upkeep.
  • Marketing and Ads: 40% of raised funds will go toward advertising the token.
  • Liquidity Pool and Exchange Listings: 50% of raised funds to secure liquidity and listing fees.
  • Founder Support: 10% for ongoing maintenance, operational support, and growth.

6. Use Cases

Donation Mechanism: Direct support to dog charities with every token sale.

Community Token: Grow your wallet while being part of a community-driven initiative.

Exchange Liquidity: Funds raised in the pre-sale will be used to secure liquidity on major exchanges.

7. Charity Contributions

1% of every transaction goes to the charity wallet, which will be donated to global and local dog shelters periodically. Contributions will be publicly viewable on the blockchain for full transparency.

8. Technology and Security

  • Built on Binance Smart Chain (BSC): Low fees and high scalability.
  • Chainlink Oracles: For fetching live price data.
  • Upgradeable Contracts: Built with OpenZeppelin to ensure long-term adaptability.

9. Roadmap

  • Phase 1: Token creation, whitepaper release, pre-sale launch.
  • Phase 2: Marketing, liquidity acquisition, exchange listings.
  • Phase 3: Partnering with global charities, NFT launch.

10. Team

Our team consists of blockchain enthusiasts, dog welfare advocates, and marketing strategists passionate about leveraging technology for impactful causes.
